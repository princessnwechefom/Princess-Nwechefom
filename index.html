About Me

I'm a Process Engineer with an MSc in Oil & Gas Engineering and Engineer-in-Training (EIT) designation from APEGA, working at the intersection of upstream energy operations, enterprise quality systems, and process optimization.

My career spans both the Alberta oilfields — where I led subsurface resource production validation programs across 25+ wells — and Ontario's industrial sector, where I designed and deployed ISO 9001:2015 quality management systems and ERP process modules from the ground up.

What sets me apart is the combination: most process engineers have either the field operations depth or the systems and quality discipline. I bring both, alongside the analytical rigour of graduate-level reservoir and process engineering research.

I'm actively working toward P.Eng licensure through APEGA and targeting Junior and Intermediate Process Engineer roles across the Canadian energy, consulting, and industrial sectors.
