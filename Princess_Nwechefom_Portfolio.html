About Me

I'm Princess Nwechefom — a Process & QHSE Engineer with a BSc in Chemical Engineering and an MSc in Oil & Gas Engineering (Distinction, 3.9/4.0) from Memorial University of Newfoundland. I am recognized for translating complex engineering and quality requirements into practical, measurable results — from achieving 100% API Q2 certification compliance for a drilling services company to driving quality efficiency improvements in fast-paced technology environments. I thrive at the intersection of technical rigour and systematic thinking, and I am committed to continuous improvement in every role I take on.
